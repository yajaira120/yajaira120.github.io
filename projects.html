PROJECT TITLE

The goal of the DyVert web application is to connect users to new media content and to provide an interactive method of bookmarking interesting content. Dyvert is a simple, comprehensive, easy to use web app with the goal of showcasing various media content from our creators to new consumers who share common interests. Our users will have 24/7 access to a social platform that lets them find and save media items (movies, shows, books) that they may want to look into based on a preview shown on a content card.
